A grounded approach to healing

I work with individuals who feel disconnected, overwhelmed, or stuck in patterns they can't seem to break. My approach combines traditional talk therapy with a deeper understanding of how our bodies hold stress.

Relational & Warm. Healing happens in relationship. I offer a space where you are met with genuine warmth.

Mind & Body. We learn to listen to what your body is telling you, reducing anxiety at its root.

At your pace. There is no rush to "fix" everything. We move at a speed that feels safe for your nervous system.
