A grounded approach to healing

I’m a clinical psychologist in private practice in San Francisco with over 25 years of experience. My specialties include women’s mental health, child & adolescent development, and parent consultation.

I earned my doctorate from the Wright Institute and my master’s from Teachers College, Columbia University. My approach is integrative and relational. I serve as your advisor and consultant, supporting you in being a good steward of your mental health. Whether you are a professional navigating burnout, a parent seeking guidance, or a teen finding their way—I am here to help you look your struggles in the eye and grow.

Adult Practice

Therapy for Women

Empowerment, education, and personal development. I work with professionals, young adults, single women, and moms pursuing connection and purpose.

Anxiety & Overwhelm

For when the "busy brain" won't stop. We work on grounding techniques, reducing rumination, and regulating your nervous system.

Relationship & Dating

Navigating difficult relationships, issues related to being single, the search for a partner, separation, or divorce.

Burnout & Balance

Managing stress, overworking, and perfectionism. Learning to "do less" and orient toward moments of appreciation.

Child & Adolescent Practice

Children, Tweens & Teens

I provide developmentally sensitive, collaborative counseling for middle and high schoolers, as well as parent consultation.

Support for Young People

The early years are fascinating but challenging. I help kids navigate:

  • Navigating anxiety, school stress, and academic pressure.
  • Managing friendships and social dynamics.
  • Coping with divorce, separation, or family changes.
  • Building identity and confidence.

* Therapy with teens respects their growing autonomy while keeping parents informed of the treatment plan.

Parent Consultation

Parenting is hard work. I provide consultation as a stand-alone service or as part of your child's therapy.
